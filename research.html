HotGauge Fine-Grained DFS

Tufts University · Advisor: Prof. Mark Hempstead

Explored fine-grained Dynamic Frequency Scaling (DFS) for mitigating advanced thermal hotspots in modern processors using the HotGauge simulation framework. The project implemented and evaluated DFS-based power trace scaling on top of a performance, power, and thermal modeling pipeline, including Sniper, McPAT, and 3D-ICE.

The study demonstrated that DFS can reduce processor power consumption and hotspot severity, while also identifying limitations such as timing distortion introduced by frequency scaling.

Keywords: Computer Architecture · Thermal Modeling · DFS · DVFS · HotGauge · Sniper · McPAT · 3D-ICE
